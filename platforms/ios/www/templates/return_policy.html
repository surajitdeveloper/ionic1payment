Return and Refund Policy

Returns


Our policy does not allow any returns. We do not process any returns on your purchases from us. This is due to the nature of our goods. We provide you names with meanings as Service and we deem the service to have been consumed as soon as the names are allocated to your account.

Refunds (if applicable)

We offer refunds in case are issues from our side due to which we could not deliver the service to you completely.

1. You paid money for one plan but names were not allocated to you at all.

2. You paid money for one plan but names from another plan were allocated to you.

3. You paid money for one plan but the promised number of names were not allocated to you.

4. You paid money for one plan but are not able to view those names in your phone due to a technical issue.

5. We would try and resolve all such issues within 2 Working days. However if we fail to do so, we will refund the charges in full on the 3rd working day if you desire.


Refunds not covered:-

1. We do not offer refunds in case you forgot your password or are not able to reset it.

2. In case you have a broken phone or screen and the app does not work properly on it.

3. In case you bought names for one alphabet or gender by mistake.


Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at contactus@uniquesikhbabynames.com or on contactus@dukantastic.com

Exchanges (if applicable)

We do not offer exchanges. Please select the name letter and gender etc carefully before proceeding to buy a plan.

Cancellation

You can cancel your order only till the time the payment has not been completely processed for it. Cancellations are not allowed after the payment has been successfully made.
